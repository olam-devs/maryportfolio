About Mary!

I’m a global citizen and lover of travel. 

I’m a content creator and social media strategist based in Dar es Salaam. My world revolves around three things: affordable adventures, authentic restaurants, and boutique hotels. I don’t just post videos and photos, I document possibilities.

Whether it’s a weekend escape on a budget or a hidden gem restaurant that doesn’t show up on Google, I’m here to show that living well doesn’t have to cost a fortune. My content is built for the curious, the intentional, and the ones who believe that every trip, big or small, is worth telling.

This space is for the explorers who want more than highlight reels. I share the real costs, the honest reviews, and the unexpected moments that make travel worth it.

So whether you’re planning your first solo trip, looking for your next great meal, or searching for a stay that feels like an experience; you’re in the right place.

Welcome to my world. Let’s explore the journeys that inspire.

Hey, I'm Mary!

My journey as a creator began in the vibrant streets of Dar es Salaam, where I spent my weekends uncovering hidden restaurant gems and proving that a great meal doesn’t need a massive price tag.

The real shift came when I headed north to Moshi and Arusha. From chasing waterfalls to experiencing safaris and discovering affordable boutique stays, I started to see travel differently. I realized that true adventure isn’t as expensive as we’ve been led to believe.

We don’t need millions to explore the world, we just need the right information and a solid plan.

Today, I’m here to bridge that gap; sharing budgets, practical tips, and honest “vibe checks” so you can stop scrolling and start exploring.

Adventure doesn’t need millions.

How I Started

For the Explorer
I believe adventure is a right, not a luxury. My mission is to remove the “expensive” barrier around travel in Tanzania by providing clear blueprints, realistic budgets, and honest vibe checks for the best hotels and hidden gems.

I do the deep-dive research so you don’t have to; so you can stop scrolling and start exploring.

For the Brand
I help hotels, restaurants, and tour operators move from hidden to high-visibility. Through cinematic storytelling and data-driven social media strategy, I create content that doesn’t just look good; it delivers results.

From driving bookings to building trust, my goal is simple: to put your brand on the map for a new generation of travelers.

My Travel Journey

  • Took my first solo abroad to Costa Rica
  • Saved up my coins with a masterplan to quit my job

2017

  • Quit my job & sold everything I own
  • Bought a backpack & one-way ticket to Mexico
  • Backpacked across Latin America & Europe solo

2018

  • Hit 10k on IG & 30k on Twitter
  • Landed my 1st paid travel collab
  • Began travel writing part-time for Elite Daily
  • Worked remotely in South East Asia and Europe
